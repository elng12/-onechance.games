The Story: 6 Days to Decide

You are scientist John Pilgrim. You and your team have accidentally created a pathogen that is killing all living cells on Earth. You have six days to find a cure. Every day is a new chance to make a choice, but also one day closer to the end.

Your choices will affect the outcome of the story. You can spend time with your family, work in the lab, or explore the world. There is no right or wrong answer, only your answer. Will you find a cure and save the world? Or will you make peace with the inevitable? You only have one chance.

How to Play

  1. Movement: Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move.
  2. Interaction: Press the Spacebar or click to interact with objects and people.
  3. Choices: Make decisions by walking to characters or objects and interacting with them.
  4. The End: The game has multiple endings. Once the game ends, it's over for good. Your choices are permanent.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the real One Chance game?

Yes, this is the original Flash game by Dean Moynihan, made playable in modern browsers using the Ruffle emulator. The experience is preserved exactly as it was.

Can I really only play it once?

The game is designed to be played only once. This is a core part of the game's message. (Technically, you can clear your browser's cache to play again, but that's not the intended experience!)

Is the game free?

Yes, One Chance is completely free to play.
